Lasagna Bolognese

A rich and hearty Italian classic made with layers of pasta, slow-cooked meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese.

-----------------------------------------------------

Ingredients:

12 lasagna noodles / 2 tbsp olive oil / 1 onion (finely chopped) / 2 carrots (diced) / 2 celery stalks (diced) / 2 cloves garlic (minced) / 500g ground beef / 250g ground pork / 800g canned crushed tomatoes / 2 tbsp tomato paste / 1 cup red wine / Salt & pepper / 1 tsp dried oregano / 500ml milk / 50g butter / 50g flour / 200g grated parmesan / 200g mozzarella (optional)

-----------------------------------------------------

Instructions:

Heat olive oil in a large pan. Add onion, carrots, and celery, and cook until softened. Stir in garlic, then add the ground beef and pork. Cook until browned. Pour in the wine and let it reduce. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer on low for at least 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally. In a separate saucepan, make the béchamel: melt butter, stir in flour, then gradually whisk in warm milk. Cook until thickened. Season with a pinch of salt and nutmeg (optional). Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then lasagna noodles, then béchamel and a sprinkle of cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with béchamel and cheese on top. Bake for 35-45 minutes until golden and bubbling. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
